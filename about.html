About Me

My Journey Into Therapy

Therapy, to me, is more than just a profession, it’s a calling that grew from my deep curiosity about people, emotions, and the ways we make sense of our experiences. From a young age, I was drawn to understanding what drives us, why we sometimes feel stuck, and how we can heal from the past. This curiosity led me down a path of learning, self-reflection, and ultimately, into the work I do today.

Throughout my journey, I’ve had the privilege of working with individuals from all walks of life, each with their own unique struggles and strengths. I’ve witnessed firsthand how powerful it can be to have a space where you feel truly seen and heard, a space where you can explore your thoughts, emotions, and patterns without fear of judgment. I firmly believe that healing happens in connection, and my role as a therapist is to create a safe, compassionate, and supportive environment where you can grow in a way that feels right for you.

Whether you're navigating anxiety, depression, self-doubt, or the impact of past experiences, I’m here to help you make sense of your emotions, uncover deeper insights about yourself, and find practical ways to move forward. My work is grounded in the belief that therapy should be a collaborative and empowering process, one that helps you not only manage challenges but also reconnect with your inner strength and potential.

If you’re feeling lost, overwhelmed, or simply ready for change, know that you don’t have to figure it all out alone.

My Approach

Therapy should feel like a collaborative, non-judgmental space where you can explore your thoughts and emotions openly.

Compassionate & Empathetic

I offer a supportive and warm environment where you feel truly heard.

Tailored to You

There’s no “one-size-fits-all” in therapy; we’ll find the approach that works best for you.

Practical & Insightful

I help you uncover deeper patterns while also equipping you with practical tools for daily life.

I draw from Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Psychodynamic Therapy, and Mindfulness-Based Techniques, blending practical tools with deep self-exploration to create meaningful change. I am also passionate about ongoing learning and growth, and I am currently pursuing an MA in Trauma and Attachment. This focus allows me to integrate the latest research on how early experiences shape our emotions, behaviors, and relationships—insights that I weave into therapy to help my clients build healthier, more fulfilling lives.

At the moment I exclusively work with adults facing a range of difficulties, from anxiety and depression to struggles with self-worth, past trauma, and relationship challenges. Life transitions—whether planned or unexpected—can stir up deep emotions, uncertainty, and stress, and I am here to help you find clarity, resilience, and a sense of direction. Whether you're feeling stuck in unhelpful patterns, seeking to understand yourself on a deeper level, or looking for tools to manage overwhelming emotions, I will meet you where you are and support you in moving forward.

Ready to Begin?

If you’re interested in working together or just have a few questions, feel free to reach out — I’d love to hear from you.
